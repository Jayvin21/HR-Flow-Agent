Job Description: Finance Automation & Management Reporting Analyst

Role Overview
We are hiring a Finance Automation & Management Reporting Analyst to support finance operations, accounting workflows, compliance tracking, MIS reporting, audit documentation, and Excel-based management dashboards. This role is designed for a commerce, CA, BMS, or finance-oriented candidate who can combine accounting discipline with reporting, coordination, and process improvement.

Responsibilities
Prepare and maintain accounting records, invoices, vouchers, ledgers, and supporting documents.
Support GST, TDS, income tax, statutory due dates, and compliance calendar tracking.
Perform bank reconciliation, accounts payable tracking, accounts receivable tracking, vendor follow-ups, and payment status monitoring.
Prepare MIS reports, budget vs actual summaries, variance analysis, payment trackers, and finance dashboards.
Build Excel-based reports using pivot tables, lookups, conditional formatting, data validation, and structured templates.
Assist with audit documentation, internal controls, approval trails, and compliance checklists.
Coordinate with internal teams for pending documents, approvals, payment status, and monthly reporting.
Improve finance processes by organizing trackers, templates, checklists, and management reporting files.

Required Skills
Financial Accounting, Management Accounting, Cost Accounting, GST, TDS, Income Tax Basics, Tally Prime, Tally ERP, Excel, Advanced Excel, Pivot Tables, VLOOKUP, XLOOKUP, Bank Reconciliation, MIS Reporting, Budget Tracking, Budget vs Actual Analysis, Variance Analysis, Audit Documentation, Internal Controls, Vendor Coordination, Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Invoice Tracking, Compliance Calendar, Management Dashboards, Business Communication

Preferred Skills
CA Foundation, CA Intermediate, B.Com, BMS, Power Query Basics, PowerPoint, Process Improvement, Finance Operations, Documentation, Stakeholder Coordination

Ideal Candidate
A finance or management-oriented candidate with strong Excel skills, accounting knowledge, documentation discipline, compliance awareness, and interest in finance operations automation.
